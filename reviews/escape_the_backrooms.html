Escape the Backrooms Review

Overview:

Escape the Backroom is short steam game that takes place the fictional location called the "backrooms". A liminal space that seemingly goes on forever often lacking something that should go in there.

Gameplay:

The game often requires you to find an exit that which there are multiple of and most of the time require you to solve a puzzle before you can actually go through the exit. The puzzles don't require or need help prior knowledge, so long as you explore the map enough you will find the information or items you need to solve the puzzle.

Rating: 7/10

  • Playtime: 17.4 Hours
  • Achievement Progress: 21/34
  • Story Progression: 100%
