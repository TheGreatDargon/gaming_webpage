Resident Evil Requiem Review

Overview:

Resident Evil Requiem is the ninth game in the Resident Evil series. It takes place at a hospital called "Rhodes Hill" and "Raccoon City" where you takes turns playing as Grace Ashcroft and Leon Kennedy.

Gameplay:

The game takes an interesting approach combining the two horror survival gameplay styles that they have been developing for the past 7 years with the release of resident evil 2 for Grace and the Resident Evil 4 Action Oriented Survival. Eventually you will make your way to Racoon City Where it leans much more into the Action Oriented gameplay and you play as Leon Kennedy. In fact, During the Raccoon city section it drops the resource management system pretty hard zombies now drop resources, and guns are upgradable through points you get through kills. I like both systems of the game so the game has been very enjoyable in my opinion, I think it is definitely worth the buy.

Rating: 9/10

  • Playtime: 11.4 Hours
  • Achievement Progress: 24/49
  • Story Progression: 75%
